About

Welcome to the Bayfront Retreat! Located bay front in the city of Jamaica Beach, this property has incredible views of Galveston Bay and is only a short golf cart ride to the beach as well! Enjoy local restaurants, food trucks and bars close by like the Way West Pizzeria, Venados Cantina, and much more - only a 10 minute drive to Galveston as well.

This home offers four bedrooms and four bathrooms in total, a large open concept living and kitchen space, plenty of entertainment area and seating and all with amazing views of Galveston Bay. There is a one bedroom suite on the ground floor, and three bedrooms and three bathrooms upstairs. Relax and enjoy your morning coffee from the third story deck while watching the kids catch fish right in the back yard, or maybe enjoy some yoga waterside on the dock. There are two kayaks, life jackets and paddles, with two bicycles and helmets available as well. An outdoor entertainment area that features a granite counter top bar, big flat screen tv, a refrigerator and ice maker, even a margarita machine for the adults - Enjoy your beverage sitting around the glass fire pit next to the water. Free high-speed Wi-Fi throughout the property, outlets and USB chargers on almost every wall and end table. Free EV vehicle charger available upon request. Washer and dryers on both the ground floor and second floor. There are also two boat slips to store your boat as well as a swim ladder if you decide to take a swim in the bay.

The Kitchen features two full size ovens, four electric stove burners, refrigerator, microwave and dishwasher. Stocked with pots, pans, baking sheet, plates, bowls, utensils, crockpot, toaster, wine glasses and much more. There is coffee and tea, offering a variety of dark and medium roasts, decaf, creamers, sugars and flavored teas. Paper towels and dishwasher detergent are also provided.

The Master bedroom is on the third floor and boast incredible views of Galveston Bay from both East and West - you won't forget these sunsets and sunrises! The calming colors make this room a destination from the hustle and bustle. There is a king bed with cooling sheets, Smart TV, dedicated work space facing the water and a walk-in closet. Attached is the master bathroom with a standup shower, jetted jacuzzi tub and his and her sinks.

The second bedroom is on the second floor and features a king size bed, flat screen TV, closet and electric shutters to help keep the room cool during hot summer months.

The third bedroom is on the second floor features two sets of bunkbeds, with reading lamps, flat screen TV, love seat and a closet.

The fourth bedroom is on the ground floor and features a king size bed and a pull out queen sleeper sofa as well. A big 55" flat screen TV is mounted on the wall, and an electric fire place with color changing flames directly below. There is a coffee bar and full size sink, a microwave, fridge and freezer and a small breakfast table that sits four people. This ground floor suite has a sliding glass door that faces the bay - great views from bed!

Amazing views of the bay whether you're cooking dinner in the kitchen or BBQing by the water. Enjoy meals or coffee on the decks while taking in the scenery and wildlife, or just lounge in the hammock by the water waiting for the fish to bite. Rent a golf cart and ride to the beach or take the bike for a ride around the neighborhood or to the community pool close by.

The city of Jamaica Beach is a short drive to Galveston and has their own fire department and police services. The community has a neighborhood park and a community pool that is $5 per person, per day - paid directly at the pool and is open from Memorial Day through Labor Day. There is also a public boat launch that require a launch pass - $12/day and can be paid and picked up at our office. Let us know if you're planning on brining your boat and we can arrange this for you prior to your arrival.

We truly hope you enjoy this piece of paradise in Jamaica Beach, and we're sure we'll see you back soon!

This property is very nice. The accommodations were perfect for our group of 9 people and we loved the location. We truly enjoyed our stay.

There are a few things that should have been addressed or clarified in the property description. The major one is the boat lift is not available to use. It would have been nice to know that we only had access to the slips as we planned on using the lift. This is NOT a no wake zone so other boaters can cause a lot of wake and our boat took a beating just being tied, even with bumpers, and not lifted. The outdoor swings were rusted out and broken. One of the kids (7yo) fell through one of them the first time he sat down the very first evening. The outdoor grill was left dirty too.

The amenities i.e., liquid hand soap and dish soap were watered down. The sponges were gross. The dishwasher soap wasn't good either and there was no laundry soap. There were no dish towels or oven mitts either. For these things, had we known, we would have brought our own but instead, spent a small fortune at the local store purchasing these items. We were only told to bring paper towels, toilet paper and trash bags so we assumed everything else would be provided.

A couple ceiling fans didn't work and there were also a few burned out light bulbs. (no biggie)

There was a foul smell in the first floor bathroom so we kept the door open the entire time.

All in all, we would stay here again for future family get togethers and know we know what we need to bring.

P.S. A blender would have been great.
